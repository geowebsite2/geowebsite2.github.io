Introduction

On the 17th of January 1995, an earthquake struck Kobe, Japan.

The earthquake measured 7.4 on the Richter scale and the earthquake occured as a result of plate movement along the boundary between Philippines Plate, Pacific Plate and the Eurasian Plate.
