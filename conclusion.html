Conclusion

After learning from the tragic earthquake and seeing how their poor crisis managment system failed and recieved major criticsm from the public and others there needed to be a way to protect the country from future disasters.

During the Post-quake rush to quake-proof the country, a steel manufacturing business reported a 10.6% increase in sales due to higher activity from quake restoration efforts and most ports were rebuilt by March 1997

4 years after the crisis the pre-quake population level does not recover for at least another 10 years.
